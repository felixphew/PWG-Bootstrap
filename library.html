Library Procedures

Guild Members may borrow and return Books and DVDs from the library at any time. The Library is usually open during weekly Wednesday afternoon meetings and monthly Saturday general meetings. Most Group Leaders have a key.
NOTE: Some Library books have restricted loan periods and some are RESERVED for reading at the Guild premises only.

To borrow an item, complete the Loan Register (see example below) (complete blue section on return). Loan period is 30 days (maximum).
