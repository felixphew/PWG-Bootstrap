Welcome to PWG!

The Peninsula Woodturners Guild is situated on the grounds of the McClelland Gallery & Sculpture Park, 390 McClelland Drive, Langwarrin, Victoria, Australia.

Our aim is to foster friendship and develop skills through our common interest in woodturning.
